Meet Emmanuel Sekyi & Seekers Realty

Founded by Emmanuel Sekyi, Seekers Realty has been Accra's most trusted real estate partner since 2019. Based at GE-038, 2097 Abokobi Sesemi, we connect buyers, sellers, landlords, and tenants to their ideal properties with speed, integrity, and precision.
